## ğŸŒ About the Project

PingUp is built with the vision of creating a social space where users can interact without noise and complexity. The platform brings together core features of social networking such as connections, posts, stories, and chatting, but in a clean, responsive and seamless experience.

The platform allows users to connect with friends, share thoughts or photos, send messages in real time using SSE (Server-Sent Events), upload stories that disappear after 24 hours, and keep profiles updated. Everything is designed to feel intuitive and fast.

PingUp also focuses strongly on user safety and privacy. While the platform supports real-time communication and media sharing, users are encouraged not to share passwords or sensitive information in chat conversations.
